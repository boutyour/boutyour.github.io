Method stack

Each pillar of the doctoral work is supported by reproducible workflows and instrumentation.

  1. Constraint modeling: Answer Set Programming, MiniZinc, OR-Tools.
  2. Simulation-in-the-loop: Unity ML-Agents, SUMO, Gymnasium.
  3. Policy learning: Factorized attention transformers, DCPO variants.
  4. Assurance: Explainability dashboards, fairness audits, energy telemetry.
